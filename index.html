About Me

Who I Am & What I Do

A snapshot of my journey from curiosity to a career in data analytics.

I Transform Complex Data Into Clear Business Decisions.

I’m a Data Science undergraduate and Data Analyst passionate about transforming raw data into meaningful insights. My journey began with a curiosity for numbers and problem-solving, which gradually evolved into a deep interest in analytics, business intelligence, and data storytelling.

Through academic learning and hands-on projects, I have developed experience with Microsoft Excel, SQL, Power BI, Tableau, and Python to analyze data, build interactive dashboards, and uncover trends that support decision-making. I enjoy working with complex datasets, identifying patterns, and presenting findings in a way that is both visually engaging and easy to understand.

Beyond technical skills, I believe that data becomes valuable only when it tells a clear story. Whether analyzing sales performance, customer behavior, financial trends, or operational metrics, my goal is to bridge the gap between data and business strategy. I continuously explore new technologies, analytical techniques, and real-world challenges to grow as a data professional and create solutions that deliver measurable impact.
